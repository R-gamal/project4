Sharm El-shaikh

Mon, May 25th 2020
Here the magic of marine and desert nature converges. It is the charming city of Sharm El Sheikh, which is unique in the beauty of coral reefs under the depths of the Red Sea and the splendor of mountain ranges and valleys, which makes you confused to divide the time of your visit between all the pleasures offered to you by the city that combines romance and an atmosphere of adventure and excitement.

Marsa Matrouh

Mon, May 25th 2020
The city of Mersa Matruh is a major Egyptian tourist resort where European tourists go, and this city is a busy trading center for Bedouins in the Western Desert, due to them being allowed to bring their own sheep and wool as well as agricultural products of dates, olives and watermelons to the local market.

Dahab

Mon, May 25th 2020
Dahab is a tourist city located in the South Sinai Governorate in Egypt, and it is the most famous after the city of Sharm El-Sheikh. Dahab is located on the Gulf of Aqaba at the Red Sea, southeast of the Sinai Peninsula, and about 140 km away from the Palestinian city of Umm Al-Rashrash.

Saint Catherine

Mon, May 25th 2020
St. Catherine has a special beauty and elegance, as it is considered one of the most beautiful and sacred cities in Sinai. Because of the important religious sites it possesses, the city also contains a group of famous mountains, which many people come to in order to enjoy the views of the sunrise and sunset on the tops of these mountains.
